Polynomial Equivalence Problems Section

If you are given two polynomials that are equal to each other, then the corresponding coefficients must be equal. This is a very useful property in numerous problems.


5x2 -17x + 13 = 5x2 + (4 - b)x + 13
-17 = 4 - b
b = 21
