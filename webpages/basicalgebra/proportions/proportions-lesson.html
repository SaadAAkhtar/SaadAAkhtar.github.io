Proportions Section

Proportions are two fractions that equal one another. The first fraction represents one scenario and the second fraction represents a second scenario. The numerators (tops) of each fraction measure one quantity while the denominators (bottoms of each fraction measure another quantity.


After setting up the proportions and setting them equal to each other, the unknown value can easily be solved for by cross multiplying.


If 24 magazines stack to be 5 inches tall, how tall will a stack of 40 magazines be?



Warm Up Question Explanation

This explanation is for the first warm up question. Because 1 inch represents 3 feet, the actual dimensions of the room are
3 × 3.5 = 10.5 feet and 3 × 5 = 15 feet. Therefore, the floor area of the room is 10.5 × 15 = 157.5 square feet, which is choice D.
